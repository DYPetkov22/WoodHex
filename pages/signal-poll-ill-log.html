Tree diseases:

Diseases include diseases caused by fungi, bacteria or viruses that affect trees. They can cause wilting, warping and other damage.

Tree pests:

Pests are organisms such as insects and animals that cause damage by attacking trees. These include bark beetles, caterpillars, rodents and others that can cause defoliation and even tree death.
